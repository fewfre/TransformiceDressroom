Transformice Dressroom

This is a free fan-made tool to allow people to create the perfect look for their mouse to share with friends without needing to own the item.

This dressroom was made for use by the Transformice Wiki in retrieving images.
However, it has been expanded to be a full-fledged easy to use dressroom.
Please enjoy!

Code based off of the cheese.formice dressroom made by Baffler.

Feel free to chat and post links to your outfits below! Please flag any inappropriate comments or spam.
